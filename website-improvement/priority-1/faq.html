Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start using The Pigsty.

Getting Started

How quickly can I set up my farm account?

Most farmers can complete setup in under 5 minutes. Create your account, add your farm, and register your first pigs. From there, you can start logging health, weight, and feed events immediately without technical training.

Do I need a laptop, or can I use a phone?

You can use both. The Pigsty is built for mobile and desktop so farm workers can record events in the field while owners review reports from anywhere. A smartphone is enough to start.

Can I import existing records from spreadsheets?

Yes. The Pigsty supports spreadsheet import so you can move from Excel to structured digital records. This saves setup time and helps your team avoid duplicate data entry.

Can I track more than one farm?

Yes. You can manage multiple farms by selecting the current farm context and switching between them when needed. This is useful for owners supporting farms in different locations.

What if I only have a few pigs right now?

The free tier is built for smallholders and new farms. You can start with a small herd and scale your records naturally as your operation grows.

Features

What records can I track for each pig?

You can track identification details, breed, stage, pen, health observations, weight logs, breeding events, and lifecycle status changes. This gives you one reliable history per animal.

Can I track breeding and farrowing details?

Yes. You can capture serviced dates, expected milestones, and farrowing outcomes. This makes breeding performance easier to monitor and helps improve planning for future cycles.

Does the app help with feed monitoring?

Yes. Feed modules let you log purchases and usage so you can monitor consumption patterns and costs. This helps identify inefficiencies early and protect margins.

Can I generate reports from farm data?

Yes. Reports summarize key farm activity so you can review trends, share updates with stakeholders, and support better weekly and monthly decisions.

Is there a way to record team activity or accountability?

Yes. Team access and activity-oriented workflows reduce confusion about who updated what. This improves operational visibility and supports stronger farm discipline.

Pricing & Billing

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. The Pigsty offers a free plan so smallholder farms can get started without upfront cost. You can upgrade when you need more advanced capacity and workflows.

When should I move to a paid plan?

Upgrade when your herd size, reporting needs, or team workflows outgrow the free tier. Most farms upgrade when better coordination and deeper analysis become operational priorities.

Do you support different payment options?

Online card checkout is supported where available. If your region has payment constraints, contact support to discuss alternatives such as regional methods where possible.

Can I cancel a paid plan anytime?

Yes, you can cancel according to the active billing terms. Your farm data remains important, so always export records before making major account changes.

Data & Privacy

Who owns my farm data?

You do. Your farm remains the data owner. The Pigsty processes records to deliver the service and does not claim ownership of your operational information.

Is my data secure?

The platform uses modern security practices including encrypted transport and access controls. Security is continuously improved to protect farm operations and account access.

Can I control who sees my farm data?

Yes. Team permissions and role-based access help you control who can view or edit records, reducing accidental changes and unauthorized access.

Can I export my records if needed?

Yes. Export options are available so you can keep backups, perform offline analysis, or share reports with partners and advisors when required.

Technical

What internet speed do I need?

A normal mobile or broadband connection is usually enough for daily usage. For smooth workflows, keep your browser updated and use stable connectivity during bulk imports.

How can I contact support?

You can reach support through the website contact options and provided channels like email or WhatsApp. Include your farm name and issue details for faster help.
